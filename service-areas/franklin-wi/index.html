Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in Franklin, WI

Built for Franklin's suburban patios, gardens, and outdoor spaces

Franklin's suburban neighborhoods offer some of the best backyard spaces in the Milwaukee area. With generous lot sizes and a strong sense of community, Franklin homeowners tend to invest in their outdoor living — and that makes it a perfect match for handcrafted furniture that's built to be enjoyed year after year.

Whether you're in one of the developments near Whitnall Park or along the Ryan Road corridor, your outdoor space deserves better than the thin-board patio sets from the chain stores. We build furniture with thicker lumber, hand-selected for grain and strength, finished with protective stains that hold up through Wisconsin winters and humid summers alike.

Franklin families often come to us looking for garden structures — raised beds for vegetable gardens, planter boxes with trellis walls for climbing plants, and decorative pieces to anchor a flower garden. We also build plenty of patio sets and swings for the entertaining crowd. Franklin's backyard barbecue culture is alive and well, and our furniture is built to be right in the middle of it.

What Franklin Homeowners Are Building

Top requests from Franklin include raised garden boxes with trellis walls for climbing plants and vegetables, A-frame swings with Wisconsin-themed carved cutouts like deer and wildlife, patio conversation sets for deck and patio entertaining, and decorative garden pieces like our solar light posts. Custom sizing is always available to fit your specific space.
