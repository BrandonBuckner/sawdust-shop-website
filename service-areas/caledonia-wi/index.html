Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in Caledonia, WI

Built right here in Caledonia — quality woodworking for your backyard

Caledonia is home base for The Saw Dust Shop. Our workshop is right here in the community, which means your furniture doesn't travel far to reach your backyard. Caledonia's spacious lots and growing neighborhoods make it an ideal place for outdoor living — and we've been building patio sets, swings, and garden structures for local homeowners who want something better than what the big-box stores offer.

Whether you live near Four Mile Road, along the Highway 38 corridor, or in one of Caledonia's newer subdivisions, we can build outdoor furniture that fits your space. From a complete patio conversation set for summer entertaining to a raised garden box for your vegetable garden, every piece is built by hand with thicker lumber and a protective stain finish.

Popular projects for Caledonia customers include full patio seating sets with custom carved details, raised garden boxes with trellis arches for climbing plants, and A-frame yard swings that make a backyard feel complete. We also build decorative pieces like wishing wells and custom mailboxes for homeowners who want something unique.

What We Build for Caledonia Homeowners

Our most requested items in Caledonia include wooden patio sets with matching chairs and tables, A-frame swings with carved cutout designs, raised garden boxes in various sizes, decorative wishing wells, and fully custom one-off projects.
