Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in Oak Creek, WI

Quality wooden furniture for Oak Creek patios, porches, and backyards

Oak Creek is one of our closest neighbors, and we've built plenty of outdoor furniture for homeowners throughout the city. From the established neighborhoods near Drexel Town Square to the newer developments along Puetz Road, Oak Creek families are investing in their outdoor spaces — and handcrafted furniture makes all the difference compared to flat-pack alternatives from the big stores on South 27th Street.

Oak Creek's mix of ranch-style homes with generous backyards and newer builds with patio spaces makes it a great fit for our work. We've built everything from full patio conversation sets for summer cookouts to butterfly-carved A-frame swings that become the centerpiece of a backyard.

If you're looking to upgrade your patio or add something special to your backyard in Oak Creek, we'd love to help. Whether it's a sturdy picnic table for family dinners, a porch glider for lazy weekend mornings, or a custom bar setup for entertaining, we build it by hand with wood that's thicker and more durable than retail options.

Popular Projects for Oak Creek Homes

Oak Creek customers often request patio furniture sets with cup holders and matching chairs, A-frame swings with custom carved designs like butterflies or deer, and raised garden boxes for vegetable gardens. We also build custom pieces for garages and outdoor entertaining areas.
