Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in South Milwaukee, WI

Durable wooden furniture built for South Milwaukee's lakeside living

South Milwaukee's character — the tight-knit neighborhoods, the Lake Michigan shoreline, the pride in doing things the right way — makes it a natural fit for handmade outdoor furniture. Many South Milwaukee homes have porches, patios, or small yards that benefit from well-built furniture that doesn't take up too much space but holds up through every season.

We've built pieces for South Milwaukee homeowners looking for porch swings that can handle the lakefront wind, benches that serve as both seating and conversation starters, and compact patio sets designed for smaller backyard spaces. If you live near Grant Park or anywhere along the bluffs, you know outdoor furniture needs to be built tough — and that's exactly what we do.

South Milwaukee customers appreciate craftsmanship — there's a tradition of working with your hands here, and our furniture reflects that same ethic. Every joint is solid, every board is thick, and every stain is applied to protect against the elements. Whether you want a yard swing for your kids to enjoy or a custom bench that shows off your personality, we build it to last.

What South Milwaukee Customers Are Building

Popular requests from South Milwaukee include A-frame swings with custom carved designs, porch gliders for covered front porches, custom benches with unique character like our Chevrolet tailgate bench, and compact patio sets sized for smaller yards. We also build raised garden boxes for the growing number of backyard gardeners in the area.
