Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in Cudahy, WI

Solid wooden furniture for Cudahy's porches, patios, and small yards

Cudahy is a blue-collar community where people appreciate things that are built well and built to last. The compact yards and welcoming front porches throughout Cudahy's neighborhoods are perfect for handcrafted pieces that don't need a lot of space to make a big impression. A porch glider on the front stoop, a small patio set in the backyard, or a raised garden box along the fence line — these are the kinds of projects that make a Cudahy home feel complete.

We build furniture sized to fit real yards — not showroom fantasies. If you need a two-seat swing instead of a four-seater, or a single raised garden box instead of a full set, we can customize the build to match your space and your budget. Every piece uses the same thick lumber and careful construction whether it's large or small.

Cudahy customers also love our custom builds — we've done bars with matching stools for basement and garage setups, decorative pieces for front yards, and personalized items that make great gifts. If you've got an idea, we can probably build it.

Projects That Fit Cudahy Living

Popular items for Cudahy homes include porch glider swings with cup holders for front porch relaxing, compact patio sets sized for smaller backyards, wooden bars with matching stools for basement and garage entertaining, raised garden boxes for side yards and fence lines, and decorative garden pieces. We deliver throughout Cudahy and surrounding areas.
