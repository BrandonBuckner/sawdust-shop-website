Handcrafted Outdoor Furniture in Racine, WI

Wooden furniture built for Racine's lakeside patios and backyards

Racine sits right on Lake Michigan, and its homeowners know what outdoor living is all about. From the historic neighborhoods near Monument Square to the residential areas along the lakefront, Racine has some of the best patio and backyard spaces in southeast Wisconsin. That's where we come in — building handcrafted furniture that matches the quality of the setting.

We've delivered patio sets, swings, and decorative pieces to Racine customers who wanted something more substantial than the thin-board furniture from chain stores. Our pieces are built to handle the lake-effect weather that Racine gets, with thicker wood and protective stain finishes that hold up season after season.

Racine homeowners often tell us they want outdoor furniture that feels permanent — something heavy enough that it won't blow around in the wind off the lake, and attractive enough to complement the yard they've put work into. That's exactly what handcrafted means: built solid and built to look good for years.

Favorite Projects for Racine Homes

Racine customers frequently request A-frame swings with dark stain finishes and custom cutout designs, decorative wishing wells for front yards and garden areas, patio seating sets for lakeside entertaining, and custom builds like our wooden bar with stools for outdoor party spaces. We also build raised garden boxes for Racine's active gardening community.
